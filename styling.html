Your guide to jean styles
Unsure which cut of jeans to buy? Here's our cheat sheet.

Skinny

Fits tightly on your legs with fabric that's often stretchy

Straight

Straight leg all the way down and the waist height may vary

Wide

A slouchy type of jean that is loose around the hips and thighs and has a wide leg

Relaxed

It features a mid-rise waist, is loose-fitting through the leg and may feature distressed or ripped denim

Cropped

The jean length rests above the ankle if you are average height but if you are shorter, these may end up being the perfect length

Mom

High waisted, baggier around the hips, tapering down to the ankle and maybe rolled up at the hem

How to style jeans

Although there are no rules for how you wear your denim, we have three up-to-the-minute styling suggestions for whatever jeans you choose

CHIC LOOK

A t-shirt tucked into jeans, worn with an oversized blazer and boots, loafers or a point toe heel.

THAT GIRL

A corset top with loose baggy jeans.

SOFT MINIMAL

A cropped cardigan or knit with high-waisted flares or straight leg jeans.
