Fashion Designing is one of the fastest growing industries in India. There is always on demand for good fashion designer in India. But getting required exposure for the same is difficult, especially for the young designers. Fashion Runway is going to be India’s best hunt for a fashion designer. The aim of this event is give Young & Professional Designers access to a new platform, as well as the opportunity to keep marketing their creation.
